There's no DC++ library ... yet. Arnetheduck has plans to turn the DC++ core 
into a library called libdcpp in the future, but for now you just have to copy 
the dc++ core folder (client/ in our source, changed to dcpp/ in latest dc++ 
svn) to your project and use it that way. Hope that helps.
